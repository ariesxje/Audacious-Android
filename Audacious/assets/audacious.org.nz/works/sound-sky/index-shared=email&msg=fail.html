Sound Sky Workshop

Artists: Trudy Lane and Halsey Burgund
Where: The Pallet Pavilion, 70 Kilmore Street courtesy of LIVS
When: March 1 and 2, 10.00am—12.00pm
Register: For Saturday March 1, for Sunday March 2.
Bring:not required to participate but additional iOS devices, preferably loaded with the free Sound Sky iOS app are welcomed.

Come and collectively create a city-wide, location-sensitive audioscape for Christchurch. Voices and music heard via a custom smartphone app reveal thoughts, memories, private imaginings left in locations around the city. For this workshop Trudy and Halsey and  project partners CEISMIC, Gap Filler, and the Cantabrian Society of Sonic Artists (CSSA) invite you to inform and contribute to the construction process for the work.

Thanks to  Creative NZ for their support of the development of this work and to Aotearoa Digital Arts for their support of a public workshop series. See all Partners.
