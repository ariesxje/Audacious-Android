The Audacious Festival of Sonic Arts features various sonic installations across Christchurch central city linked by ‘sound walks’ led by sonic artists at regular intervals throughout each day. Rally points for sound walks are near the Bridge of Remembrance in Cashel Street Mall and the Audacious booth in Cathedral Square
